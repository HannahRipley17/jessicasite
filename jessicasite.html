Hi! My name is Julie! I love to organize, and I love photos! PhotoJewels2U is a fairly new company ran single-handedly by me, Julie Stipe. I recieved certification in mentorship and photo organization by the A.P.P.O. (Association of Personal Photo Organizers) in 2018 and have used that to aid people in overwhelming photo organization tasks ever since, even though I have been doing photo organizing since 2008. I started a passion for photo organization as my late mother and I decided that making memories last forever needed to be a priority and we worked together scrapbooking and digitizing our thousands of photos for years until she passed. Now I have more passion then ever as I carry on our work together and strive to bring my talents to others so they too can make lasting memories a priority. I believe that photos are gems that allow us the unique ability to catch glimpses of moments in time and to see into other's lives. I strive to make these gems that can keep our memories alive for generations to come available to as many people as I can. Hi! My name is Julie!
